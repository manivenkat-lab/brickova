# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date: April 02, 2026**

## 1. Information Collection
We collect data to provide a premium real estate experience:
- **Profile Info**: Name, contact details, role.
- **Listing Data**: Property address, images, title deeds.
- **Financial Data**: Transaction IDs (we do not store cards).
- **Network Data**: IP address, device type, location.

## 2. Information Use
- To facilitate property verification and matching.
- To process secure payments via our banking partners.
- To improve Platform performance and analytics.
- To provide customer support and service updates.

## 3. Information Protection
- Data is encrypted in transit and at rest.
- Access to sensitive user data is restricted to verified Bricova personnel.
- All protocols are regularly audited for security.

## 4. Third-Party Integration
- **Google Cloud**: To host the Platform and provide AI services (Gemini).
- **Razorpay**: To provide secure payment gateways.
- **Firebase**: For real-time database and authentication.

## 5. Sharing Data
- We do not sell your data to third parties.
- Data is shared with legal authorities only if required by law.
- Your contact info is shared with verified leads upon your explicit consent.

## 6. Access and Deletion
- Users can request a copy of their data at any time.
- To delete your account and associated data, contact our support protocol.

## 7. Protocol Updates
This policy may be updated periodically. Significant changes will be announced via the Platform dashboard.
