# Cookie Protocol

**Active Version: 1.4 (April 02, 2026)**

## 1. Protocol Purpose
Bricova uses cookies and similar technologies to enhance Platform performance, security, and user experience.

## 2. Essential Cookies
- **Auth Token**: To keep you signed in securely.
- **Session ID**: To track your current listing draft and navigation.
- **CSRF Token**: To prevent cross-site request forgery attacks.

## 3. Performance & Analytics
- **Google Analytics**: To understand how the Platform is used.
- **Mixpanel**: To track interaction patterns and improve the lead protocol.

## 4. Personalization
- **Recent Searches**: To show you properties based on your past interests.
- **Region Detection**: To pre-select cities with high listing density.

## 5. Third-Party Cookies
- **Payment Providers**: Razorpay may set cookies to process payments and verify transaction security.
- **Map Providers**: Google Maps may store cookies to render maps and compute distances.

## 6. Managing Logic
- You can disable cookies in your browser settings.
- Note: Disabling essential cookies will render the Platform inoperable.

## 7. Duration
- **Session Cookies**: Deleted after you close the Platform.
- **Persistent Cookies**: Remain on your device for up to 365 days.

## 8. Questions
For more info, contact our support protocol (support@bricova.com).
